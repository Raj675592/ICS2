About ICS IITK

Welcome to the IIT Kanpur Counselling Service (ICS), where we prioritize your mental well-being. Our dedicated team of professionals is committed to providing you with the support and guidance you need to navigate life's challenges. Whether you're a student, faculty member, or staff, we're here to help you achieve a balanced and fulfilling life.

Our mission is to create a safe and supportive environment where you can explore your thoughts and feelings, develop coping strategies, and enhance your overall quality of life. We believe that seeking help is a sign of strength, and we are here to empower you on your journey towards mental wellness.

For more information about our services, please explore our website or contact us directly. Your mental health matters, and we are here to help you every step of the way.
